Bienvenue sur eStudium

E-Studium is a basic front-end for the AusoHNum-library.

AusoHNum is the center for Digital Humanities of the Institute Ausonius, a joint research unit (UMR 5607) of the CNRS and the University of Bordeaux Montaigne. Its members are involved in research as well as teaching activities, and take part to many of the projects run at the Institute, all along the full process of research: data acquisition, processing, enrichment, analysis and publication. Beside an internationally recognized expertise in databases related to humanities and social sciences, AusoHNum is also specialized in geomatics and 3D modeling applied to archeology.
